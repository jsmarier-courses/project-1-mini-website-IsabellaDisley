The Midday Market

Unfortunately, the Midday Market's last event is this upcoming Saturday from 11am-6pm. This specific event is hosted in Chinatown at 687 Somerset Street West. Not to worry, there are many upcoming events and announcements to be presented soon!

The Fly Market

During the summer, they would host this event in the Byward Market. From 11am-5pm, the Fly Market is hosted at 581 Bank St, also known as the store, Top of the World! There are more announcements to follow in the near future as well!

613Flea

In the heart of Lansdowne, at the Aberdeen Pavilion, the 613Flea market is open! This event runs from 10am-4pm every Saturday. During the summer, it would be held outdoors beside the pavillion and indoors during the winter!
